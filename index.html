Cyber-Guard

Developed at the ITWeb Summit Hackathon, CyberGuard is a comprehensive anti-phishing solution designed to prevent phishing scams and protect users from fraudulent and unsafe websites. The project features an email scanner that detects phishing attempts and a custom browser extension that continuously scans websites visited by the user. It flags potentially harmful sites and recommends safer alternatives to enhance online security.
